History

White Water Rafting Company was founded with a passion for adventure and a love for the outdoors. Since the early days, our mission has been to bring people closer to nature while providing unforgettable rafting experiences.

Over the years, we have expanded our services, trained the best guides, and developed routes for all levels. Safety, fun, and respect for nature are our top priorities.
